MOBILE APP DEVELOPER (SENIOR/JUNIOR)

  • Reference Number: DEV01
  • Salary Range: ~20.000.000VNĐ - 35.000.000VNĐ
  • Report back to: Lead Software Engineer

Key Responsibilities

  • Make prototypes, user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Conduct user research and testing to gather insights on user behavior and preferences.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure designs are implemented accurately and efficiently.
  • Translate complex concepts into clear and intuitive user experiences.

Required Skills

Essential:

  • For Senior Role: Minimum 10 years of experience in mobile app development.
  • For Junior Role: Minimum 1 year of experience in mobile app development.
  • Proficiency in programming languages: Swift (iOS), Kotlin (Android), and Java.
  • Strong knowledge of API integration and working with RESTful services.
  • Familiarity with version control tools such as Git.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Preferable:

  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team.
  • Experience with backend development in Node.js or Firebase.
  • Familiarity with UI/UX design principles.

UI/UX DESIGNER

  • Reference Number: DES01
  • Salary Range: ~17.000.000VNĐ - 24.000.000VNĐ
  • Report back to: Lead Product Designer

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and prototype user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Create wireframes, user flows, and mockups to effectively communicate design ideas.
  • Conduct user research and testing to gather insights on user behavior and preferences.
  • Stay updated on current UI/UX trends, tools, and best practices to continuously improve designs.

Required Skills

Essential:

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in UI/UX design.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles and interaction design.
  • Excellent communication skills and attention to detail.

Preferable:

  • Knowledge of design systems and ability to maintain UI consistency.
  • Familiarity with animation tools (e.g., After Effects) for creating micro-interactions and transitions.
  • Familiarity with HTML/CSS principles.
